Image Generating AI (I.G.A.I) WebsiteGeneral Description
Pixray Text2ImageThe OG AI Image Generator. This website displays the progress as an image is generated, AND you can scroll through all the past frames with a slider. I used this feature to (painfully) make .gifs out of a single prompt.
Stable DiffusionThe most popular website on this list. Produces up to 4 images, but can have a pretty long queue, if you're unlucky.
Diffuse the RestYou get to draw on a canvas to guide the ai to make what you want (You still need a prompt)
CrAIyonFormerly known as "DALL-E Mini", this somewhat formerly viral website generates NINE images at a time*! AND, this site is the first one I found that knew what "among us" is! *(these images, however, are pretty low-resolution)
Composable DiffusionAllows you to combine several prompts into one image, and control the weight of each prompt on the final image. If an error keeps appearing, you probably forgot to change the weights section after changing the number of prompts.
Min Dall-eBasically CrAIon, but upgraded in every possible way except for the lack of dark mode. You can generate up to TWENTY FIVE images at one time, and it (on average) takes LESS TIME than CrAIon takes to generate nine. Plus it has settings for you to mess around with.
Outpainting with Stable DiffusionAllows you to upload an image, and the ai will try to extend it. However, the ai works a lot better with natural landscapes, and will tend to create mountains and forests from any arbitrary image.
